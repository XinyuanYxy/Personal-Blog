A little story about myself

I love playing guitar. I started taking guitar classes when I was in grade 8. But I had to cancel the course when I had finished 15 classes because the music school was about a 1-hour drive from home. Every time I had to go there all by myself, which had discouraged me a lot. But I picked up the guitar again as the first semester in university began. I have been learning guitar on my own ever since. I watch tutorials to learn skills, tricks and theories.

I have been reading The Great Gatsby recently. I really enjoy reading this book. I know I should have read it by the end of high school. But it's never too late to be a book lover.
